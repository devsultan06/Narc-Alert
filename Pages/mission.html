Our Mission

At Narc Alert, our mission is to provide a supportive platform for survivors of narcissistic abuse to register their abusers, warn others, and receive and give the support needed to rebuild their lives. We are driven by the belief that no survivor should suffer in silence, and that by sharing our experiences, we can create a powerful network of empowerment, healing, and prevention.
