Insight Into Our Vision

Welcome to Narc alert - a platform dedicated to supporting survivors of narcissistic abuse and fostering a community of strength, healing, and empowerment.
At Narc alert, we understand the devastating impact of narcissistic abuse and the long-lasting effects it can have on survivors. Our mission is to provide a safe space where survivors can register their abusers, warn others, and receive and give the support they need to reclaim their lives.
