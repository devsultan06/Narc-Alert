What is narcissist abuse?

Narcissistic abuse refers to a pattern of harmful behavior inflicted upon individuals by someone with narcissistic traits or a diagnosed narcissistic personality disorder (NPD). It is a form of emotional and psychological abuse that can have severe and long-lasting effects on the victim's mental, emotional, and sometimes physical well-being.

Narcissistic abusers often exhibit traits such as grandiosity, a sense of entitlement, a lack of empathy, manipulation, and a need for excessive admiration and attention. They employ various tactics to exert control and power over their victims, including gaslighting, manipulation, emotional manipulation, belittling, criticism, isolation, and exploiting the victim's vulnerabilities.
